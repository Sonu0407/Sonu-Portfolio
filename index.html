Game Developers

A game developer is a software engineer, programmer, or scientist who designs, tests, and maintains video games12345. They may work on the code that makes up the game's engine, on the game's AI, or on the graphics and animation1. They may also work on the game's level design or on its audio and sound effects1. Game developers can have a hand in conceptualizing a game, creating assets like graphics and sounds, writing code that runs the game, improving the user experience for players, continuously testing the game, and much more2.
